Ingredients:
200 ml of water
10 ml of lemon
10 ml of lime
20 mg of sugar

Instructions:
1. Take 50 ml of water and boil it to 100 degrees. Prepare sugar.
2. Add sugar to 100 degree water, stir it and put inte a fridge. Let it cool down.
3. Take another 150 ml of water and add lemon and lime.
4. Take sugared water and add it to lemon-limed water as much as you want. I add the whole cup.
